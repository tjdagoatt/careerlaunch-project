# Problem Statement

Many students struggle to manage the career preparation process because job applications, resumes, deadlines, notes, and skill-building plans are often spread across multiple platforms. This can lead to missed deadlines, disorganization, and poor follow-up. CareerLaunch solves this problem by providing a centralized system where users can organize applications, track progress, and develop career-related skills in one place.

## Target Users
- College students
- Recent graduates
- Career advisors

## Value
The system improves organization, visibility, and accountability during the job and internship search process.
